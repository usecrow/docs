## How It Works

1. **Add the widget** — Drop a script tag into your website
2. **Configure your AI** — Set up your product knowledge in the Crow Dashboard
3. **Identify users** (optional) — Enable personalized experiences for logged-in users
4. **Go live** — Your users can start chatting with your AI agent
